01 — About Us

A supplier that thinks like the engineer on the other end of the line.

Crown Fluid Systems is a Mysore-based supplier of refrigeration and hydraulic components, serving HVAC&R technicians, hydraulic workshops, OEM fabricators and industrial maintenance teams across Karnataka and neighbouring states.

We focus on the parts that keep a system sealed and under control: service valves and charging valves for refrigeration circuits, and fittings and valves for hydraulic power lines. Every item we stock is selected for pressure rating, material quality and fit — because a failed valve doesn't just cost money, it costs downtime.

What sets us apart is straightforward: real stock on the shelf, technical staff who understand the application (not just the part number), and a buying experience built for people who need an answer the same day, not a quotation next week.

02 — Product Range

What we supply

Four core lines, each stocked for immediate dispatch. Ask us for datasheets, pressure ratings or bulk pricing on any item.

Refrigeration Service Valves

Ball-type and diaphragm service valves for suction, discharge and liquid lines — built to hold rated pressure across repeated service cycles on AC and cold-chain systems.

  • Brass & forged-body ball valves
  • Angle & straight-through service valves
  • Flare, solder and threaded connections

Refrigeration Charging Valves

Schrader-type and core charging valves for safe, leak-free refrigerant charging and recovery — the small parts technicians rely on to get the gauge connection right every time.

  • Schrader charging valves & cores
  • Access fittings for HVAC&R systems
  • Replacement valve cores & caps

Hydraulic Fittings

High-pressure adapters, couplings and connectors engineered for hydraulic circuits where leak-free sealing under shock load is non-negotiable.

  • BSP, NPT & metric adapters
  • Hose-end couplings & quick connects
  • Reducers, tees & union fittings

Hydraulic Valves

Directional, flow-control and relief valves for hydraulic power packs and mobile equipment, matched to working pressure and flow requirements.

  • Directional control valves
  • Pressure relief & flow-control valves
  • Ball & needle valves for hydraulic lines

Related Accessories & Spares

Gauges, seals, gaskets, O-rings, manifolds and consumables to round out every installation or repair — so a single order covers the whole job, not just the headline part.

  • Pressure gauges & manifolds
  • O-rings, gaskets & seal kits
  • Hose clamps & brass nipples
